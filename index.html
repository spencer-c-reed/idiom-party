👅 TIP OF THE TONGUE
🤪

Sounds wrong.
Feels right.

Read the nonsense aloud until your team hears the hidden saying.

How to play

  1. Split into two teams and pass one phone around.
  2. The reader keeps the screen hidden and says the nonsense aloud. Their teammates solve it by sound.
  3. Tap Got it! for a point or Skip. You can reveal the answer if everyone’s stuck.
  4. When time runs out, pass the phone. First team to the target wins.
