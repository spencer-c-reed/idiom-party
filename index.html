👅 TIP OF THE TONGUE
🤪

Sounds wrong.
Feels right.

Decode absurd synonym-swapped clues into familiar sayings before time runs out.

How to play

  1. Split into two teams and pass one phone around.
  2. On your team’s turn, someone from the other team holds the phone and reads the distorted clue. Your whole team guesses.
  3. Tap Got it! for a point or Skip. You can reveal the answer if everyone’s stuck.
  4. When time runs out, pass the phone. First team to the target wins.
